Group Ticket Qty — WpEvently Add‑On

Enable customers to purchase multiple tickets in groups with flexible quantity selection options.

Overview

With the Global/Common Qty Addon, the WooCommerce Event Manager Plugin's ticket quantity can be managed as a common or global quantity. By default, quantity works per ticket type, but this addon allows you to manage inventory across all ticket types together.

Settings

After installation, navigate to the event edit page. You will find settings to enable common/global quantity management. Instead of tracking stock per ticket type, the addon uses a single pooled quantity for the entire event.

Purchase

This addon is available for purchase at the MagePeople website.
