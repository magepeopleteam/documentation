Overview

The Event Email Reminder Addon sends automatic email reminders to event attendees. You can also send review emails or any other communication before or after the event.

Settings

You can set up a maximum of 3 reminders per event. Each reminder can be configured with its own timing (before or after the event). The addon also keeps log details so you can view the sending history for each event.
