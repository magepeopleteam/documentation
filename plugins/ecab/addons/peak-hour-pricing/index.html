Taxi Peak Hour Pricing — eCab Add‑On

Set dynamic fares during peak hours to maximize revenue. Customize rates for different times of the day and make pricing simple and efficient.

Overview

Optimize pricing during peak hours to maximize revenue. Automatically adjust fares based on demand and time of day to ensure profitable operations.

Configuration

Go to the eCab settings page and locate the Peak Hour Pricing section. Define peak hour time windows (e.g., 7:00–9:00 AM, 5:00–7:00 PM) and set a multiplier or fixed surcharge for each window.

Usage

When a booking falls within a defined peak hour window, the surcharge is automatically applied to the fare at checkout.
