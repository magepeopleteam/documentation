Distance Tier Pricing — eCab Add‑On

Charge accurately with tiered pricing based on distance. Create flexible fare rules to match short, medium, or long rides and increase your earning potential.

Overview

Charge accurately with tiered pricing based on distance. Create flexible fare rules to match short, medium, or long rides and increase your earning potential.

Configuration

After installing the addon, navigate to the eCab settings panel where you will find the Distance Tier Pricing section. Define distance ranges (e.g., 0–5 km, 5–15 km, 15–30 km) and set a fixed or per-km price for each tier.

Usage

When a customer books a ride, the system automatically calculates the distance and applies the corresponding tier pricing during checkout.
