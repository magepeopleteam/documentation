Multi‑Day Discount Pricing — WpRently Add‑On

Offer special discounts for multi-day rentals. Encourages longer bookings and helps increase overall rental value while giving customers better pricing options.

Overview

Offer special discounts for multi-day rentals. Encourages longer bookings and helps increase overall rental value while giving customers better pricing options.

Configuration

Navigate to the WpRently settings panel and locate the Multi-Day Discount Pricing section. Define day ranges (e.g., 3–5 days, 6–10 days) and set a percentage or fixed discount for each range.

Usage

When a customer selects a rental duration that falls within a defined multi-day range, the discount is automatically applied during checkout.
