## Overview

Reward early bookings with special discounts. Perfect for tours that sell fast, this addon encourages travelers to reserve seats quickly, boosting your revenue.

## Installation

1. Download the addon zip from your account.
2. Go to **Plugins → Add New → Upload Plugin**.
3. Upload the zip and click **Install Now**.
4. Activate the plugin from the Plugins page.

## Usage

1. Go to the tour edit page and enable **Early Bird Pricing**.
2. Set the early bird discount period (number of days before the tour).
3. Define the discount amount or percentage.
4. Early bird pricing is automatically applied during checkout within the configured period.
