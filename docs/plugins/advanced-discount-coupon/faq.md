# FAQ

## Can I create BOGO deals?

Yes. Buy One Get One Free and other BOGO variations are supported.

## Can coupons be limited to specific products?

Yes. Coupons can be restricted to specific products, categories, or exclude certain items.

## Does this work with subscription products?

Yes. Advanced Discount Coupon works with WooCommerce subscriptions.

## Can I set coupon expiration dates?

Yes. Coupons can have start and end dates, and auto-expire.

## Is Elementor supported?

Yes. Elementor widgets for coupon display are included.
