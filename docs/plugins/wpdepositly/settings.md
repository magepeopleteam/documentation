# Settings

## General Settings

- **Default Deposit Type** — Percentage or fixed
- **Default Deposit Amount** — Default deposit value
- **Enable Full Payment Option** — Allow customers to pay in full

## Payment Settings

- **Payment Deadline** — Days until balance is due
- **Reminder Timing** — When to send payment reminders
- **Late Fee** — Charge for overdue payments

## Display Settings

- **Deposit Label** — Custom label for deposit option
- **Show Breakdown** — Display payment breakdown at checkout
- **Balance Due Notice** — Show remaining balance on account page
