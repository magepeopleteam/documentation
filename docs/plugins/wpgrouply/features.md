# Features

## Group Booking

- Minimum and maximum group size
- Per-person pricing model
- Group leader registration
- Participant details collection

## Pricing

- Per-person pricing
- Tiered group discounts
- Child/infant pricing
- Group package pricing

## Participant Management

- Add/remove participants
- Collect individual participant details
- Participant list export
- Communication with group leader

## Availability

- Group slot availability
- Simultaneous group booking handling
- Capacity management per time slot
