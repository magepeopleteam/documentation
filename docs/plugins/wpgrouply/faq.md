# FAQ

## How does group pricing work?

Pricing can be per-person or flat rate, with optional tiered discounts for larger groups.

## Can group members have different ticket types?

Yes. You can configure different pricing for adults, children, and infants.

## How are participants managed?

The group leader provides participant information during booking. This can be customized per plugin.

## Does WPGrouply work with all MagePeople plugins?

Yes. WPGrouply is compatible with WPEvently, WPTravelly, and other MagePeople plugins.

## Can I set minimum group sizes?

Yes. Minimum and maximum group sizes are configurable in settings.
