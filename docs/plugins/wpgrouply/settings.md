# Settings

## General Settings

- **Min Group Size** — Minimum participants per group
- **Max Group Size** — Maximum participants per group
- **Default Group Pricing** — Per-person or flat rate

## Pricing Settings

- **Group Discount** — Percentage discount for groups
- **Tiered Pricing** — Different pricing for different group sizes
- **Child Discount** — Reduced pricing for children

## Form Settings

- **Collect Participant Details** — Enable/disable per-participant forms
- **Required Fields** — Required participant information
- **Group Leader Fields** — Additional leader information
