# FAQ

## Can I rent both products and vehicles?

Yes. WPRently supports any type of rental item — vehicles, equipment, tools, etc.

## How does security deposit work?

Deposits can be configured as a percentage or fixed amount, collected at booking and refundable upon return.

## Can I set different prices for weekends?

Yes. Weekend, weekday, and seasonal pricing can be configured.

## Is inventory tracking included?

Yes. Real-time inventory tracking prevents double bookings and shows accurate availability.

## Does WPRently work with WooCommerce subscriptions?

WPRently integrates with WooCommerce for payment but does not require subscriptions.
