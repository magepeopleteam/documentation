# Pricing Discount Over x Days Add-On

Offer discounts to your customers for longer rental periods. Automatically apply tiered discounts as the rental duration increases, encouraging customers to book for more days.

## Installation

1. Visit the [Pricing Discount Over X Day Addon page](https://mage-people.com/product/pricing-discount-over-x-day-addon-for-rental-and-booking-plugin/) and click **Purchase Now**.
2. Select your preferred plan and pay with ease.
3. You will receive your addon download link shortly.
4. Go to your WordPress dashboard and navigate to **Plugins → Add New → Upload Plugin**.
5. Upload the Over_x addon zip file and click **Install Now**.
6. After installation, click **Activate Plugin**.

## Usage

1. After activation, go to any of your Rental Item settings.
2. Scroll down to find the **Discount Price Over x-Days** settings section.
3. Toggle the setting to enable it.
4. Add your Discount over days, Discount Type (percentage or fixed), and the Amount.
5. Add more discounts over X days by clicking **Add New Discount**.
6. Click **Update** — the discount is automatically applied at checkout when the rental duration reaches the threshold.
