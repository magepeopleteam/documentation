# FAQ

## Can I create multi-day tours?

Yes. WPTravelly supports multi-day tours with a built-in itinerary builder.

## Does it support partial payments?

Yes. You can configure deposit payments with a final payment deadline.

## Can customers book for a group?

Yes. Tour booking supports group sizes with per-person pricing and tiered rates.

## Is Elementor supported?

Yes. WPTravelly includes Elementor widgets for tour listings, search forms, and booking.

## How does seasonal pricing work?

You can set different pricing for peak and off-peak seasons directly in the tour settings.
