# Features

## Discount Types

- Percentage discount
- Fixed cart discount
- Fixed product discount
- Buy one get one free (BOGO)
- Tiered volume discounts

## Rule Conditions

- Cart subtotal minimum/maximum
- Product and category restrictions
- User role-based discounts
- Purchase history conditions
- Date and time restrictions

## Dynamic Coupons

- Auto-generated coupon codes
- Single-use and multi-use coupons
- Per-customer usage limits
- Coupon combinations rules

## Promotions

- Flash sale scheduling
- Seasonal campaign management
- Cart-based auto-discounts
- Free shipping coupons
