# FAQ

## Can I have multiple staff members?

Yes. WPBookingly supports unlimited staff with individual schedules and service assignments.

## Can customers reschedule online?

Yes. Customers can reschedule or cancel bookings through their dashboard, subject to your policies.

## Does WPBookingly send reminders?

Yes. Automated email and SMS reminders can be configured.

## Can I take deposits?

Yes. Configure deposit payments for bookings.

## Is Elementor supported?

Yes. WPBookingly includes dedicated Elementor widgets.
