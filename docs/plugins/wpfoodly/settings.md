# Settings

## General Settings

- **Order Slug** — Custom URL slug
- **Currency** — Set pricing currency
- **Time Format** — 12-hour or 24-hour
- **Weight Unit** — Measurement unit

## Restaurant Settings

- **Default Location** — Primary restaurant location
- **Operating Hours** — Business hours per day
- **Delivery Radius** — Maximum delivery distance
- **Preparation Time** — Estimated prep time for orders

## Order Settings

- **Min Order Amount** — Minimum order for delivery
- **Max Order Distance** — Maximum delivery distance
- **Order Cut-off** — Cut-off time for immediate orders
- **Schedule Orders** — Enable advance ordering

## Payment Settings

- **Payment Gateways** — Available payment methods
- **Cash on Delivery** — Enable/disable COD
- **Service Fee** — Additional service charge
