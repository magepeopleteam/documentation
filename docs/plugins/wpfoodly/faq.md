# FAQ

## Can I manage multiple restaurant locations?

Yes. WPFoodly supports multiple locations with individual menus and hours.

## Does it support delivery and pickup?

Yes. Customers can choose between delivery and pickup at checkout.

## Can I set delivery zones?

Yes. Delivery zones and fees are configurable per location.

## Does it work with Elementor?

Yes. WPFoodly includes Elementor widgets for menu display and ordering.

## Can customers schedule orders in advance?

Yes. Advance ordering can be enabled with configurable time slots.
