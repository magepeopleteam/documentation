# FAQ

## Can customers select their seats?

Yes. An interactive seat map lets customers choose their preferred seats.

## Does it support multiple stops per route?

Yes. Routes can have multiple boarding and drop-off points.

## Can I manage cancellations?

Yes. Cancellations can be processed with configurable refund rules.

## Is Elementor supported?

Yes. WPBusTicketly includes Elementor widgets for booking forms and search.

## How are e-tickets delivered?

E-tickets are sent via email as PDF attachments after successful payment.
